About our small little Cafe

Welcome to Café Leblanc, a cozy haven nestled in the heart of Sangenjaya. Our café is more than just a place to enjoy a cup of coffee; it's a sanctuary where you can unwind, relax, and savor the rich flavors of our signature coffee and curry, both crafted with love and care by our owner, Sojiro Sakura.

  • Join us at Café Leblanc, where every visit is a new experience, and every cup of coffee steals a part of your heart.
  • At Café Leblanc, we provide a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Enjoy our delicious coffee and hearty curry in a friendly, inviting environment.
  • Discover the charm of Café Leblanc in Sangenjaya. Our cozy ambiance, delicious coffee, and savory curry make us the perfect place to unwind and enjoy life's simple pleasures.

At Café Leblanc, we believe in creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where every guest feels at home. Whether you're here to catch up with friends, dive into a good book, or simply enjoy a moment of peace, our café is your perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Leblanc Coffee

Brewed with care, served with comfort

The Café Leblanc Coffee is a warm, inviting cup of richness that greets you with a faint aroma of freshly roasted beans. As you bring the mug closer, the scent is a deep, earthy blend—there's the subtle sweetness of caramelized sugar from the roasted beans, mingled with the smokiness that only comes from a well-aged, high-quality brew

Charles Special Curry

A comforting blend of warmth and spice that will steal your heart after the first bite

Charles Special Curry is a comforting dish that warms both body and soul. As the rich, savory aroma fills the air, the deep scent of slow-cooked spices and tender meat promises a meal that’s both hearty and satisfying. The curry is a thick, velvety sauce, a perfect balance of mild heat and aromatic spices. There’s a hint of sweetness from a well-balanced mix of ingredients, with just the right touch of heat to give it depth, but not overpower the flavors.

Leblanc Tiramisu

The moment you take a bite, the creamy mascarpone layer melts in your mouth, smooth and velvety with a hint of vanilla and a slight tang that cuts through the sweetness

The coffee-soaked ladyfingers, soft and spongy, provide a deep, aromatic flavor, their bitterness complementing the sweetness of the cream. A dusting of cocoa powder on top adds a slight bitterness that contrasts beautifully with the rich, sweet layers beneath. Each bite offers a harmonious blend of textures—the creamy, the soft, and the slight crunch from the cocoa. It’s a dessert that feels both comforting and luxurious, with every element coming together to create a flavor profile that lingers pleasantly, leaving you craving just one more spoonful.

Joker's Dark Brew

A Night with the Master is a refreshing and unexpected fusion of fruity sweetness and the bold richness of coffee

The drink is bold, intense coffee blend known for its strong, robust flavor profile. The coffee often has a deep, dark roast that delivers a rich, smoky taste, with an earthy undertone.
