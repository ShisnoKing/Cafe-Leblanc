About our small little Cafe

Welcome to Café Leblanc, a cozy haven nestled in the heart of Sangenjaya. Our café is more than just a place to enjoy a cup of coffee; it's a sanctuary where you can unwind, relax, and savor the rich flavors of our signature coffee and curry, both crafted with love and care by our owner, Sojiro Sakura.

  • Join us at Café Leblanc, where every visit is a new experience, and every cup of coffee steals a part of your heart.
  • At Café Leblanc, we provide a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Enjoy our delicious coffee and hearty curry in a friendly, inviting environment.
  • Discover the charm of Café Leblanc in Sangenjaya. Our cozy ambiance, delicious coffee, and savory curry make us the perfect place to unwind and enjoy life's simple pleasures.

At Café Leblanc, we believe in creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where every guest feels at home. Whether you're here to catch up with friends, dive into a good book, or simply enjoy a moment of peace, our café is your perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

WHY US

Why Choose Our Cafe

01

Cozy atmosphere

Café LeBlanc offers a warm and inviting environment where customers can relax and unwind. Whether you're catching up with friends or seeking a quiet place to read or work, our cozy interior with comfortable seating and ambient lighting makes it the perfect spot to enjoy some downtime.

02

Exceptional Coffee

Our café prides itself on serving high-quality coffee made from carefully selected beans. Each cup is brewed to perfection by our skilled baristas, ensuring a rich, flavorful experience every time. Whether you're a fan of classic espresso or love trying seasonal specialties, our menu has something to delight every coffee lover.

03

Friendly Service

At Café LeBlanc, we believe in making every customer feel like a valued guest. Our staff is known for their friendly and attentive service, always ready to accommodate your needs and provide personalized recommendations. We strive to create a welcoming community atmosphere where everyone feels at home.
